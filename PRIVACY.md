Privacy Policy for BlinkFit

Last updated: March 2026

BlinkFit is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how we handle your data.

No Data Collection
BlinkFit does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No account is required, and no data is ever transmitted off your device.

Camera Usage
The front camera is used only during exercises (Blink, Stretch, and Palming) to detect blinks, eye movement, and hand positions. All camera processing happens in real time on your device. No video, images, or frames are recorded, saved, or uploaded. Camera access is optional; if denied, the app will still function with manual timer alternatives.

Local Data Storage
All app data (exercise sessions, eye status, settings) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device. You can delete the app to remove all stored data.

Third-Party Services
BlinkFit does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy
BlinkFit does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app means acceptance of any changes.
