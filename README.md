## 📱 About BlinkFit

BlinkFit guides you through four science‑backed exercises to reduce digital eye strain:

- **Blink** – Camera‑counted blinks with fruit‑themed targets.  
- **Stretch** – Follow prompts to look up, down, left, and right.  
- **Palming** – Rub your palms, then cover your eyes (camera or timer).  
- **Break** – A 20‑20‑20 style rest timer.

You can run a full session (Blink → Stretch → Palming → Break → Review) or jump straight to a quick activity from the home screen.  
All data stays on your device; the camera is used only during exercises and **no video is ever recorded or stored**.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Camera unavailable
- Go to **Settings** → **BlinkFit** → turn **Camera** **On**.  
- If the camera still doesn’t appear, restart the app or your device.

### Blink not detected
- Ensure good lighting and face the screen directly.  
- If you wear glasses, try removing them (or enable **one‑eye mode** in Settings).  
- The app uses your front camera; make sure nothing is covering it.

### No sound
- In BlinkFit’s **Settings**, check that **Sound** is **On**.  
- Also verify your device’s silent switch is off and volume is turned up.

### No haptics
- Go to BlinkFit **Settings** → **Haptic** → **On**.  
- Haptics are only available on devices that support Core Haptics.

### Reminders not showing
- Enable notifications in **Settings** → **BlinkFit** → **Notifications** → **Allow Notifications**.  
- Also turn on **Reminders** inside the BlinkFit app’s Settings.

### Tips section empty
- Quit the app (swipe it up from the app switcher) and reopen it. The tips should reload.

### App feels slow or stuck
- The first launch loads assets – this is normal.  
- On later launches, close other apps if needed, or restart your device.

### Palming says “hands lost”
- Keep both hands in view of the front camera.  
- Ensure your hands are well lit and not too close or too far.  
- You can also use the **timer option** if camera detection fails.

BlinkFit respects your privacy:
- The camera is used **only** during exercises and **never records or saves** any image or video.
- All app data (sessions, settings, eye status) stays **on your device**.
- No accounts, no cloud storage, no tracking.
